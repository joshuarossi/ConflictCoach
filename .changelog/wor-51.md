### WOR-51: Draft Coach panel for private message drafting

Added the Draft Coach — a private, AI-assisted message-crafting experience embedded in the joint chat view. Participants can open a side panel (or full-screen bottom sheet on mobile) from the "Draft with Coach" button, privately discuss what they want to say with the AI coach, and receive a polished draft. The DraftReadyCard presents four actions: Send this message (posts to joint chat), Edit before sending (drops text into the chat input), Keep refining with Coach, and Discard.
